Skype for Business (previously known as Lync) is a tool for communication and collaboration between teams within company.

It consists of instant-messaging client and Skype for Business Server.

It is very powerful productivity tool for those companies who have Skype for Business standardization, providing them with combination of instant messaging, video conferencing and files sharing.
