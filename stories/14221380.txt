Hannah is the founder of Trendeing.com, and is passionate about helping others find the beauty in themselves and confidence in who they are. Hannah's first introduction to filmmaking was as a composer when she wrote a couple pieces of music for short films. Hannah has worked in TV and film for the past eight years and is passionate about continuing to learn more about media in our culture today.|||
