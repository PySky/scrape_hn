Onshape is the first and only full-cloud 3D CAD system that lets everyone in a design team work together using any web browser, phone, or tablet.

Onshape was built from scratch for the way today’s engineers, designers and manufacturers really work, giving them secure and simultaneous access to a single master version of their CAD data without the hassles of software licenses or copying files.

Based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, Onshape is venture-backed and it’s team consists of key members from the original SolidWorks team plus elite engineers from the cloud, data security and mobile industries.|||
