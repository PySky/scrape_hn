Gearogs will host listings for synthesisers, turntables and more.

Discogs is opening a marketplace for audio equipment. Gearogs was launched in early 2016 as a database for all kinds of audio equipment. Currently there are categories for synthesisers, turntables, DJ mixers, drum machines, mixing desks, tape machines and more besides. In an interview with, Discogs CEO Kevin Lewandowski said that Gearogs will become a marketplace in April, meaning users can list, buy and sell products listed in the database. Gearogs is just one of numerous Discogs spin-offs such as Bibliogs for books, Comicogs for comics and Filmogs for movies.|||
