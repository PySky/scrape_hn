Mental Calisthenics are exercises that you can do by yourself or with a partner. The intent is to exercise different faculties of the brain and induce a meditative state of mind.

(2) is a game for two. The rules are that one person starts by saying a word. The following person must then choose a word that begins with one of the previous word’s last two letters. The first player must then do the same for this word, and loop back and forth like this. Words cannot be reused. The first person to be unable to think of a matching word loses.
