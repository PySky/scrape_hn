Lady Light combines dance with laser manipulation. In this piece she "fights" the laser then effortless bends and throws it around. One of the most complex and theatrical laser manipulation acts around. Stunning tableaus and unique choreography.



Music: "Dreaming" by BT

Choreography, programming and performance by Lady Light
