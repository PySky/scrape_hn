Realmpusher is my submission for the Ludum Dare 38 compo, and I'm pretty proud of it! The game isn't easy to figure out at first, but I like the mechanic and I'm happy with how quickly and efficiently I produced everything.

Push the blocks around in the center room, and they'll rearrange the corridors around you. Connect them in the right way to find all the magic orbs. Make your dad proud!|||
