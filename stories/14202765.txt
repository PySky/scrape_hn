With the help of the open source Telerik JustDecompile Engine , the compared assemblies are decompiled to C# and then compared. Decompilation to VB.NET is also possible, but not yet available. To all contributors: feel free to add it to the project.|||

JustAssembly is a .NET assembly diff and analysis tool which allows you to decompile and compare two versions of an assembly with a click of a button.
