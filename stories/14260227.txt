Chaos was among us after WhatsApp went down last night.

And people all over the world were unable to send messages to their friends.

The service went down across Europe, the US, Brazil and Mexico.

Users were faced with the ‘timer of doom’ when they attempt to send a message – and at the top of the app it continuously said ‘connecting’.

A spokesman for WhatsApp told Metro.co.uk: ‘WhatsApp is aware of the issue and working to fix it as soon as possible.’|||
