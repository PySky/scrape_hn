

 The Stelvio Pass located in Italy is one of the most dangerous roads in the world. It is a road that’s only open between june to september, due to the ruthless weather in the area. The road is situated within the eastern Alps and it is 9000 ft above the peak, and 15 miles in length. Out of the four months the road is open, it’s ideal to keep you focused, since one minor mistake or miscalculation can be fatal, especially if you are going over the speed limit.
