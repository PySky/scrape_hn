Launch your app on NodeChef with a single ‘deploy’ command or upload your project folder from the dashboard. The NodeChef runtime orchestrates and manages the execution and scale of your apps.

Nodechef provides zero downtime vertical and horizontal scalability. Containers can be added and removed on demand with a single click of a button.

NodeChef supports startup bash scripts. You can leverage this feature to install any ubuntu package or custom binaries.|||

NodeChef Node.js hosting platform is based on managed containers, with integrated MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Redis and powerful add-ons, for deploying and running your apps.
