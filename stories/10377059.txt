Legal expert Michael H. Cohen joined Pat Salber (@docweighsin) at MedX on the Stanford Campus to talk about understanding when a medical device or app falls under the purview of regulation (FDA or FTC) and when it does not. Some of the issues that separate regulation from non-regulation are subtle and are related to the words that are used to promote the technology. Watch the video to learn more.
