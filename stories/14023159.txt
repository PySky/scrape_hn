If you think the rent is too damn high, there are a couple of startups that want you to prove it, by forcing you to bid for a place to live. You may find out you’re right. Or you may discover you aren’t offering enough.

San Francisco-based Rentberry allows tenants to bid, eBay-style, on the rent, security deposit and other negotiables of any rental property.
