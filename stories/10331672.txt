Main features:



+ Discover professionals available for a meeting nearby and in the next 7 days.

+ See professional's background.

+ Request a meeting with them in 1 tap.

+ Create value by adding your own Open Office Hours.

+ See who wants to meet you.

+ Accept or decline meeting requests.

+ Chat with your connections directly in the app

+ Find the people you already know importing your professional contacts

+ Grow your professional network adding the people you meet to your connections

+ Add yourself to the waitlist of users you would like to meet



Other features:



+ Optionally import your information from your LinkedIn account

+ Edit your profile: customise it to match your networking goals

+ Repost meetings you already created: no need to re-enter all the information.

+ Messaging: exchange details or updates about your meetings with your counterparty.

+ Maps integration: use your favourite maps app to see the meeting location and get directions.

+ Block users: ideal for users that are too persistent or to hide from your boss or co-workers.

+ Invite your relevant connections to join the Smartcasual community
