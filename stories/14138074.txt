Business Process Analysis is a methodology for the analysis of a business with a view to understanding the processes and improving the efficiency and effectiveness of its operations.

1.Identifying process hiccups in existing workflows and analyzing them for improvement

2.Suggesting ideas on reducing or eliminating the redundancies found in the processes

3.Documenting process data and using it as reference for future decisions

4.Business process analysis can help organizations make the most of their limited resources, cut costs, and offer better values to their products and customers
