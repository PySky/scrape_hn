Mental Calisthenics are exercises that you can do by yourself or with a partner. The intent is to exercise different faculties of the brain and induce a meditative state of mind.

(9) is a game for two players. To start, one person thinks of a word. The other person then starts asking questions with the intent of guessing the secret word. The first person should guide the person to their word, however they are only allowed to respond with color names. Questions regarding the spelling of the word are disallowed.

This game is a miniature simulation of what happens along language boundaries. What emerges is often not really a combination of either side’s native language but rather something called a domain specific language: a language that does not have meaning outside of its small context.|||
