Instmap allows you to see Instagram photos nearby or anywhere in the world.

 Do you know how many interesting people are around you right now?

Instmap is easy as 1, 2, 3.

 1. Go to your location or anywhere in the world .

 2. Press to load photos from the selected position.

 3. Share amazing places or even embed them in your website.

Aim your Instmap crosshair wherever you want, hit the button and see what's going on there!

Instmap never store your location or any other personal data. Instmap is not associated with Instagram. Contact developer.|||
