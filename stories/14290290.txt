Colin Breck works as an engineer at Tesla, building streaming-data systems for the near real-time monitoring and control of distributed, renewable-energy resources. His interests lie in the performance, reliability, and security of these systems. He has almost two decades of experience in developing publish-subscribe infrastructures and time-series databases for industrial applications.
