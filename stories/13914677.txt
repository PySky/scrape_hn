cron.weekly is a weekly newsletter, delivered to you every Sunday, with news & tools tailored to Linux sysadmins.

It features new Open Source projects, guides & tutorials, news and handy little CLI one-liners. cron.weekly is here for both junior as well as seasoned Linux users.

The focus of the newsletter is on technical content. Interested what that looks like? Take a look at the archives.

Got a news item I should definitely include in the mailing? Let me know via mail or on Twitter!

Alternative methods to get the content: RSS feed or via the @CronWeekly Twitter account.|||
