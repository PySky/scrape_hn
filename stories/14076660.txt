# Origin:

The Ultimate writing tool to go from an idea to the first draft. A simple, minimalistic, distraction-free, typewriter styled writing tool, that lets you just write and save plain-text files.



#Features:

- Open

- Write

- Save

files with .txt, .md, or .markdown extensions.

- 4 different themes.

- iCloud Support.



#Things you can't do in Origin:

- Scroll.

- Undo / Redo.

- Cut / Copy / Paste.

- Formatting the text.

- Any sort of editing in the already written text.

- Printing the document.



For best experience use fullscreen mode.|||
