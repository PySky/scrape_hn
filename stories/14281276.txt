Last week, I received a friend request from someone on Facebook. After reviewing his profile, I figured we have some common friends, so I said: “OK, maybe he wants to ask me or something”.

Some minutes later, he sent me an invitation to like his page. I visited the page and found: one post and two status (“changed the cover” & “changed profile picture”). Without hesitation, no like was given and I deleted him from my friends list.

Don’t get me wrong, I’m not that type of guy who don’t want to help others, absolutely not. But I don’t want to be part of a dumb way of measuring success: likes.|||
