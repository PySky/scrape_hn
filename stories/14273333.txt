One of our favorite lead generation sayings is that within each sale, two sales actually take place: the selling of the appointment, and the sale of the service. Appointment setting, thus, is the first “sale,” and is a crucial step in the purchasing process.

Successfully setting a sales appointment is just as important as selling the product itself. Adjusting and improving your team’s appointment setting techniques will not only make the rest of the sales process easier — it will result in a higher number of closed deals and increased revenue at the end of the day.|||
