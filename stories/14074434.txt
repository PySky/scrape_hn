CWU Geology's Nick Zentner presents 'Wenatchee: Ice Age Floods". Topics include the bedrock of the Wenatchee area, and evidence for Ice Age floodwater that crept up the Wenatchee River Valley as far as Leavenworth. Record on June 5, 2013 at Ellensburg City Library's Hal Holmes Center in beautiful downtown Ellensburg, Washington.|||
