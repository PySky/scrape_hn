One of the big mysteries of Deep Learning is, how do we apply this disruptive new technology to improving our business? There are plenty of questions that are quite open to be able to answer this question. Which business process shall I apply Deep Learning to? Is it even feasible to apply Deep Learning to my selected context? Will it be worth the effort? How exactly will I go about implementing this? These are just some of the questions that pop up. It is indeed a complex subject and we definitely are in dire need of some kind of framework where we can organize our thinking around this. That’s what a this canvas is for.
