Some websites serve different versions of their pages depending on the webbrowser detected. This can result in an entirely different page for "GoogleBot" than for the average user. Wallfluxallows you to control what kind of webbrowser websites assume you are using.

Add this bookmarklet UA to your bookmarks-bar to send any webpage directly to Wallflux UA.|||
