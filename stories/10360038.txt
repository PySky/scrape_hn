Sales of Apple Macintosh computers slowed to its lowest rate in two years, industry research firms reported on Thursday.

The research firms Gartner Inc. and International Data Corp. disagreed on the precise degree of the slowdown. IDC reported that Apple shipments shrank 3.4% year-over-year, while Gartner reported 1.5% growth.   Apple shipped 5.6 million units, according to Gartner; IDC counted 5.3 million units.|||
