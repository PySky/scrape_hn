Google search that we all know, is working on a tool called "Google Hire", which is a job applicant tracking system that appears to rival services.

The tool lets employers post job opportunities, accept, verify and manage applications. Some companies have been testing and using the tool, companies like SingleHop, DramaFever, Medisas, CoreOS and Poynt that we know of.



This tool will help reduce the corruption and work load of the job processes.
