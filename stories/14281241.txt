Robert Bosch GmbH has won orders from Apple Inc. to supply the next iPhone with some of its motion sensors. According to Patently Apple, Bosch’s sensors have properties that Apple plans to bring to future products related to Augmented Reality, in-air gesture recognition, indoor navigation and smart glasses.
