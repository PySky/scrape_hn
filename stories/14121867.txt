Two late Portuguese shepherd children were canonized by Pope Francis in a large outdoor mass in Fatima, Portugal on Saturday, becoming the youngest saints in Catholic history who were not martyrs. The children, Francisco and Jacinta Marto, died of the Spanish influenza at ages ten and nine, and are known within the church for receiving visions of the Virgin Mary.
