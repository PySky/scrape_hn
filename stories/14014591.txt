Costrak timesheets is online time tracking software. This time tracking system would completely replace most paper based timesheet systems. As a web based application , it is ideal for organisations where the work force is widely dispersed, and time tracking is required. The workers on the move can submit timesheets from the field whenever they have internet access.The system enables you to export your data to csv format at any time.There are two versions of the online time tracking timesheet system available. The professional services firm version , which is best suited to users who want a detailed breakdown of work done. Then there is the contractor/temp agency version , where the focus is on total time per day, and billing the client efficiently.|||
