Molten is a suite of graphics development frameworks that give you the power to transform your graphics applications and games on iOS and macOS platforms. Whether you want to move to the next-generation Vulkan API on iOS and macOS, or simply want to run your OpenGL ES 2.0 application or game faster on iOS or macOS, Molten has a graphics framework that will help you get what you want!

Molten includes MoltenVK, an implementation of Vulkan on iOS and macOS, plus MoltenGL, a faster implementation of OpenGL ES 2.0 on iOS and macOS.
