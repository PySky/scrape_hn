The art of drawing on a computer has become something of a lost art since Microsoft Paint's heyday, but Google's new program is hoping to bring it back.

AutoDraw, a new website from the internet giant, uses artificial intelligence to recognise what you are drawing, and suggests a professionally-made version, turning your amateurish sketches into works of art.

When you begin drawing something, AutoDraw starts to recognise what you're trying to accomplish from your crude doodles, and can turn them into drawings that actually look something like the real thing.|||
