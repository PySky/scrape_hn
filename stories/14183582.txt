You might know that Infinite Loop is name of the street encircling the Apple Campus in Cupertino, California. You probably also know it’s the name of Apple’s new circular offices (opening to employees this year).

Now though, it’s also the name of a new exhibition by Turner Prize nominees Ben Langlands and Nikki Bell.

With striking pieces referencing the offices of IBM, Facebook and the Gates Foundation, this explores the futuristic architecture of our global internet giants.|||

Infinite Loop: Turner Prize artists expose the world's most exciting digital headquarters - from Apple to Facebook.
