Some retailers are offering a free PlayStation 4 camera with the purchase of a PlayStation VR headset. The camera is valued at $59.99 when bought separately and is required to track a player’s position in the room.

The $399.99 bundle can be found at Amazon. It’s unclear whether this bundle is promoted by Sony or for how long the promotion will be offered.

Sony’s PlayStation VR is one of the most popular headsets in the market so far. Earlier this year, Andrew House, global chief executive of Sony Interactive Entertainment said, as of February 2017 consumers have purchased 915,000 PlayStation VR headsets, roughly four months after it went on sale.|||
