Helio is a lightweight, minimalistic tool, that helps you to get into the zone and focus on music.

Hosts VST2, VST3 and AudioUnit. Runs on CoreAudio, ASIO, DirectSound, ALSA, JACK and OpenSLES. Imports and exports MIDI. Renders to WAV, FLAC and OGG.

Helio introduces version control-driven workflow. The point of version control is overcoming the fear of making changes to your work. Every changeset can be tracked, every revision can be restored.

Built-in color schemes and translations to English, German, French, Italian, Spanish and Russian.|||
