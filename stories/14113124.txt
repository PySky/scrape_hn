In the race to put self-driving vehicles on the street by 2021, major automakers and suppliers are partnering with, investing in and acquiring smaller component makers and technology start-ups so that they can expand their capability, pool expertise, share the work load, cut cost and get the cars to market sooner. But the car companies remain divided on how much self-driving development and integration to farm out to the parts makers, or whether to keep most of that in-house.
