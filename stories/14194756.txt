ICQ offers integration with the IFTTT platform: now users can easily send posts and photos from other social networks and applications to ICQ live chats and channels by syncing the services.

There are currently 11 applets available: for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Medium, Tumblr, etc. In addition, users will be able to create their own applets: IFTTT supports about 400 applications and services.

In order to set up automatic sharing of posts to ICQ, a chat or channel owner needs to register with the IFTTT website, choose a template, and specify a chat. After that, each new post will be shared to the chat automatically.|||
