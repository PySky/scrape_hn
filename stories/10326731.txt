The site contains materials of a course entitled Service Engineering, taught by Professor Avishai Mandelbaum at the Technion, Israel.  The emphasis is, theoretically, on Queueing Theory, and, practically, on Telephone Call Centers and Service Networks.  The site provides access to real data, lecture notes, homeworks and references.
