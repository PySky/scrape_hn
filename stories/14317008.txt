Artillery.io is an advanced load testing toolkit written in Node.js. It supports testing HTTP, Socket.io and WebSocket-based services and can be scripted with JS. This talk will provide a walkthrough of how Artillery.io can be used to help you prepare your application for high load.



About Hassy Veldstra:



Hassy Veldstra is the creator of Artillery.io, and a DevOps and automation consultant at YLD with a particular interest in performance and chaos testing.
