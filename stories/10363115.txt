Quest Nutrition released their latest flavor of quest bar – with the season of fall in mind. Quest’s new Pumpkin Pie Flavored protein bar will satisfy your taste-buds without the excessive carbs and sugar from traditional protein bars. Quest bars are sugar free and low in carbohydrates due to there high fiber content.

Ingredients of there new Pumpkin Pie Quest bar include:

 Protein Blend (Milk Protein Isolate, Whey Protein Isolate), Soluble Corn Fiber (Prebiotic Fiber), Palm Oil, Almonds, Water, Erythritol, Pecans, Natural Flavors, Pumpkin Powder, Sea Salt, Butter, Sunflower Lecithin, Baking Soda, Sucralose, Annatto Extract (Color), Xanthan Gum, Cinnamon, Steviol Glycosides (Stevia).
