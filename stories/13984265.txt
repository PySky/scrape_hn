When hiking season is just around the corner, it's time to steer those giant snow blowers up the Lyseveien! AirVūz contributor simenhaughom documented the annual occurrence in this drone video of the Norwegian mountain road and its surrounding range. Although the snow is completely cleared from the road, towering walls of snow line this pathway that leads up to Kjerag. So, get out your hiking gear because this outdoor adventure is ready for you!|||
