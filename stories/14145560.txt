Bit is Python's fastest Bitcoin library and was designed from the beginning to feel intuitive, be effortless to use, and have readable source code. It is heavily inspired by Requests and Keras.

Bit is distributed on PyPI as a universal wheel and is available on Linux/macOS and Windows and supports Python 3.5+ and PyPy3.5-v5.7.1+. >= 8.1.2 is required.

Docs are hosted by Github Pages and are automatically built and published by Travis after every successful commit to Bit's branch.|||
