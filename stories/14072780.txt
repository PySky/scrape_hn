This video is about DMT experiences and the surprising similarities told between people, as so complex and indescribable as it is.

A total of 12 hours have been cut in 30min chronological order made out of ~30 reports, some have been recorded right after the experience.

Attempting to explain it always require the use of metaphors as it is impossible to accurately describe the experience with words.
