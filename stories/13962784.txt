Faqbot is an advanced customer support technology that transform companies's FAQs page into a conversational agent based on the web, Facebook and Messenger platforms.

We create a FAQ database that is driven by customer conversations and maintained by customer support. Faqbot continues to learn from each conversation thru machine learning to improve his responses.

Faqbot is the foundation to build different personalised guided experiences for inward or outward engagement automation ranging from customer support, sales or lead collection purposes.|||
