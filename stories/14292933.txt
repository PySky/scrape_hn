A simple console application that enables hackers to get latest soccer scores and fixtures.

 At the present moment, supports English Premier League, Spanish Primera Division and Turkish Super League.

 All data is being parsed from .

This application is pure Dart and works on only Dart VM.

Option: : Specify which league you want to get information about. Must be provided.

 Option: : Specify which team you want to get information about. Optional.

 Flag: : Get previous results or not. When used with a team name, it returns results of last 5 games.

 Flag: : Get next games or not. When used with a team name, it returns next 5 games.|||
