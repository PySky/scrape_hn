Go Online or Go out of Business

Khaleej Times featured Storat.com CEO, Mustapha Ramadan, in an article about the importance of bringing your business online and how Storat is disrupting the concept of Online Marketplaces.

You can read the full article at: http://www.khaleejtimes.com/smart-ceo-2017/storat-go-online-or-go-out-of-business

Mustapha, stressed in the article about the importance for any business in the Middle East to build a strong online presence with a clear digital marketing strategy. He also emphasized on building your own brand, and not be hidden in online marketplaces and directories.|||
