These t-shirts were inspired by all the crushed traffic cones out there on the road. People run over them with no regard. Relationships are similar. After a terrible break up we often feel like Mr. Cone. Heartbroken, in need of love, but at the same time still suffering from wounds to get too close to anyone. The irony! I hope you all enjoy this.
