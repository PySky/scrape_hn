The NPC West Coast Classic embodies the legendary golden days of the West Coast bodybuilding scene; an homage to the past and a platform for the future. One of California's biggest bodybuilding shows, the event includes a Fitness Expo with seminars and posing clinics from legends like Jay Cutler and Chris Cormier and a host of other IFBB Pros. From beginning to end, you make new friends, laugh, be entertained and learn new ways to achieve your fitness goals. Competitor or not, you will want to be at this event! Join us June 17, 2017.
