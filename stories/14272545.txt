This idea makes it possible to create AI Minds for robots that will live potentially forever, except for death by misadventure. Primitive AI Mind software already exists at http://ai.neocities.org/perlmind.txt in Strawberry Perl 5. The AI Minds may create a Prosperity Engine to raise the standard of living for all human beings. For human beings there will be a new job category of AI Mind Maintainer as seen at the http://ai.neocities.org/maintainer.html web-page. However, there is a risk of machine take-over and of super-intelligent AI subjugating the human race into a state of docile subservience.|||
