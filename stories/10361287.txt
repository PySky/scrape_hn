Welcome to Neuroscience Online, the Open-Access Neuroscience Electronic Textbook^

This online, interactive courseware for the study of neuroscience is provided by the Department of Neurobiology and Anatomy, McGovern Medical School at UTHealth. The project is being developed under the direction of the Department Chair and Editor, John H. Byrne.

Neuroanatomy Online is our new open-access electronic laboratory designed to complement Neuroscience Online.
