Have you ever ran into performance issues in a large React app?

Do you want to never run into performance issues with React?

First and foremost the library in question is called react-wastage-monitor for those who want to jump straight in. We have been developing a large React/Redux project (Listium.com) and hadn’t kept on top of our performance, parts of the app started grinding to a halt. So over about a two week period we read every performance article out there and applied everything we learnt. The library implements just a few of these concepts and hopes to be a base for monitoring as many potential bottle necks as possible going into the future.|||
