Shady Water is a short documentary in the making focusing on the plastic shade balls (HDPE) that were dumped in LA drinking water reservoirs. It has been brought up that chemicals infused in making the black plastic balls may cause cancer and and other health concerns. According to the news, Los Angeles spent $34 million dollars to dump 96 million black plastic HDPE 4 inch balls into the 4 drinking water reservoirs. Many fear there may be long term health effects from the HDPE breakdown.|||
