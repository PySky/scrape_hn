Install the SoundLogin Authenticator app on your phone and the SoundLogin extension on your desktop browser (your PC must have a microphone)

The mobile app generates one-time passwords (OTPs) or extracts OTPs from SMS and transfers them to your browser via notification sounds

In SoundLogin extension settings, choose two-factor authentication-enabled services from our list

Enable encryption in SoundLogin settings to additionally protect your mobile-to-browser acoustic channel|||
