Domain-Driven Design (DDD) can help focus and guide software development efforts, but learning DDD can be a daunting task. While DDD is deep and its implications extensive, there are three simple philosophical rules which guide both understanding and application: Capture the domain model, Embed it in the code, and Protect it from corruption. Come learn how these rules guide DDD at all scales.
