Agent-based model of users on a forum, or other thread-based platform.

Its aim is examining the impact of structural factors on thread-based platforms ability to attain critical mass.

Examples of such factors are how threads are layed out, in which order they are shown (if any), and whether new users get to see new threads first…

Still under heavy development. Ideas, comments and feedback welcome (mail@wybowiersma.net).

Open using Firefox or Google Chrome and click 'Start Simulation'.
