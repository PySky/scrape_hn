Call of Duty: Black Ops III is on top of the multi-platform list. Bethesda’s Fallout 4 is also among the leaders, as well as Assassin’s Creed: Syndicate. These games will be released on PS4, Xbox One and PC. Electronic Arts also has a couple of projects on the list, including Need for Speed (92%) and Star Wars: Battlefront (88%).

Among other anticipated titles are Halo 5: Guardians for Xbox One, StarCraft II: Legacy of the Void (PC), Anno 2205 (PC), Uncharted: The Nathan Drake Collection (PS4) and Code: Realize – Guardian of Rebirth (PS4). Most of the games are published by big companies. No indie titles are mentioned by Nielsen.|||
