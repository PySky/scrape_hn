Sony Corp. acquired Softkinetic Systems SA, a Belgian sensor-technology firm, to strengthen its image sensor and related services, the companies said Thursday.

The Japanese electronics and entertainment major didn’t disclose how much it paid for all the shares of the Brussels-based company, but said the deal’s impact on its financial results would be limited.|||
