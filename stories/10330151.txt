50 years ago today, Apollo 1 astronauts Grissom, White and Chaffee were killed in a fire on the launch pad during a countdown rehearsal. The tragedy resulted in a thorough re-thinking of astronaut safety and an extensive re-working of the Apollo command module, and well over a year delay before a manned Apollo mission would fly.
